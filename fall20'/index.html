Brand Identity

Stiftelsen Falsterbo Strand

Description

Stiftelsen Falsterbo Strand is a foundation which manages a strip of land along the beach in Falsterbo in the south of Sweden.

Falsterbo is famous for its rich history and is still today a popular destination for people from all of Sweden during summertime.

The mission of the foundation is to put the valuable land to use by lending it to locals.

Assignments

Website
Designed and developed a website for the foundation from scratch.
